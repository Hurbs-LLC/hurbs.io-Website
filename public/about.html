ABOUT HURBS

Started with one computer in 2012. Never stopped.

Hurbs is Mason Herbel's IT shop. One name on the email, a bench of programmers and IT pros across the country behind it.

photo: Mason / the shop

Hurbs started in 2012 with a pile of parts on Mason's desk and hasn't slowed down since. Today we handle IT, software, security, and networks for businesses that want their tech handled by someone who picks up.

Small enough that you always talk to Mason. Big enough to take on real work: programmers on deck when a project lands, and IT people across the country we trust.

If something can't be fixed remotely, we fly out and get it done right, priced fair. Or we hire and manage the right local hands for the contract. Either way, our name is on it.
